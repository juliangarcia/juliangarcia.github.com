Vitae

In Brief:

  • Between 1998 and 2003 I studied at the National University of Colombia, I graduated in 2004 from the program in Computer and Systems Engineering.

  • Between 2004 and 2006 I worked in the software industry. I was also an auxiliary lecturer teaching computer programming and numerical methods.

  • In 2006 I started my PhD at the Faculty of Economics, Vrije Universiteit, Amsterdam. I also spent a lot of time at CREED, University of Amsterdam. I graduated in 2011 with a thesis on evolutionary dynamics and game theory.

  • From late 2010 I am a post-doctoral fellow, working with Arne Traulsen in the Evolutionary Theory Group of the Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Biology.
